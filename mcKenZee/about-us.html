who we are

McKenzee STAT Agency is a measurement company providing data and analytics about the FMCG market. Our specialization is retail audit at the level of cities and micro-regions.

The investigated trade channel - traditional retail (outlets of types - B, C)

what we do

We do not use auditors to manually collect information at the point of sale, as traditional research companies do. By minimizing the involvement of the human factor, we ensure that our analytics are as close as possible to real FMCG sales.

The heart of the company is a team of analysts and data scientists with experience in international companies in the field of market research. Even so, we are collaborating with leading universities in the country and developing additional opportunities for R&D.
